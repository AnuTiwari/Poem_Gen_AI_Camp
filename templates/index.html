About

This poetry generator was created by a team of students at AI Camp, and this document showcases the results of their efforts. Our project aims to combine the beauty of poetry with the power of modern artificial intelligence. By leveraging advanced NLP models, we have built a tool that can generate meaningful and creative poems based on user-provided prompts.

The journey began with a deep dive into the intricacies of language models, training techniques, and the art of poetry itself. Our team collaborated to fine-tune a GPT-Neo model, a cutting-edge transformer model, which we trained on a diverse corpus of poetic works. This enabled the model to understand and generate text that mirrors the rhythm, style, and emotion found in traditional poetry.

Future Scope

This project can be further developed to assist poets struggling with writer's block by providing advanced tools and inspiration. It is intended to be an open-source, free resource for the creative community.

Conclusion

Our journey in developing the AI poetry generator has been a transformative exploration at the nexus of technology and creativity. Leveraging HTML and CSS for a polished frontend and Python, along with the GPT-Neo model from the Transformers library and various NLP tools, we immersed ourselves in NLP preprocessing and training. We successfully uploaded our trained model to Hugging Face and integrated it into our website via API, unified seamlessly by Flask. This holistic approach allowed us to not only build a functional tool for generating poetry but also to deeply engage with the intricacies of AI-driven creative expression. As we refine our algorithms and envision future enhancements, we reflect on how this project has brought us full circle, from conception to realization, in redefining the boundaries of technology-enhanced artistry.
